Project

LoF is an opensource MMORPG in development. We intend to create an all new Steampunk MMO in the near future, using CC-BY-SA and GPL content. We have no requirements for people to develop, LoF is a very open project. If you have any skills and are interested in developing for the game, let Pihro know ingame, or contact him at pihro.landoffire@gmail.com or PM him in the forums. Thank you for your interest!
