A game and a community

Meet new people and team up to fight monsters and complete quests!

Currently Land of Fire (LoF) is a mutant clone of The Mana World with new quests, monsters, and items.

The game is free to play and open source.

We use the ManaPlus client, to play on our server launch the client and choose Land of Fire from the list of available servers.

Land of Fire plans on building an entirely new Steampunk MMO, feel free to look at our project page for details and more information on development.

Join us at our IRC Channel!!!
